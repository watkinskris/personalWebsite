Kristina Watkins

Founder and CEO of Leaping Leopards Sewing
Student of Computer Science at Udemy

In the summer of 2019, while looking for gymnastics leotards for my daughter, I realized just how expensive and ill fitting they can be. I decided that I wanted to offer a more specialized product and fit, at a price that people could afford. My daughter asked to name the business, and Leaping Leopards Sewing was born. I focus on producing comfortable and stylish gymnastics leotards and dance wear.

When I am not sewing, I am focusing on improving my computer programming skills. At the moment, web development and learning the ins and outs of git and github are my main focus. In the future I hope to begin learning cyber security and forensics.
